The Rancho Deluxe Lodge

This spacious LODGE offers 2,000 sq ft. has 3 bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms. Master bedroom (El Jefe's) room w/ king size bed and master bath w/ shower and tub. Bedroom # 2 (Rio's room) has Queen bed and adjoining shower and toilet. Bedroom # 3 (Stock Yard) room has two FULL size pull down MURPHY beds. The main house has an open living room space, dining table, full kitchen and laundry room.

Sleeps up to 8 people.


Summer Rates

$450 a night for 1-6 persons (2 night minimum) We charge an additional $50.00 per person up to 8 max.

  • Add 13% hotel tax & a $100 cleaning fee
  • Discounts available 10-15%, on Sunday – Thursday rentals
  • 3 Night Minimum on Holiday Rentals: Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas / New Years.
